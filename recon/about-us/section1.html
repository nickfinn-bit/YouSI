About Us

We are the representatives of the Energy Societies from Oxford, Imperial, UCL and Cambridge. Collaborating to create ever-better opportunities for the student body.

​

In 2024 we launched the initiative, receiving incredible amounts of interest from the student body, with over 700 project applications from Imperial alone.

​

In 2025 we have been building larger project collaborations as well as developing the Sustainability Entrepreneurship Competition, which will open inaugurally in October 2025.
