Venture Support

If you have an idea for a science-based venture that supports sustainability, this is the program for you. If you are looking for partners with specialised knowledge that complements yours, we can make the connection. We connect each accepted venture with mentors, both business as well as technical experts, using our network; you will have the opportunity to meet with them every 2 weeks for guidance. More details follow for accepted applicants.

​

Our program works on a rolling basis, with presentations to Venture Capital funds and mentors when the venture has received enough support to do so.
