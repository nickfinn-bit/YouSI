Our Events

The programme will feature a mix of in-person and online sessions, offering a variety of engaging formats. These will include panel discussions with invited speakers, round-robin networking sessions with professionals, and technical talks - delivered in accessible terms so that even those with little prior knowledge can participate.

​

Themes include the merits of baseload versus responsive or entirely green energy systems, the politics of carbon capture, the carbon neutrality of biofuels, the geopolitics of oil compared to renewables, the feasibility of hydrogen as energy storage, and many more exciting topics.
